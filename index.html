eNotePad is a useful and simple notepad with many features, the main one being that you can save as much text as you like while taking advantage of the formatting options like markup languages such as Textile or Markdown give you.

This means that you can use eNotePad to jot down ideas, write an article, an essay, a collection of media (more on this later) or anything your heart desires.Onine at any time through your mobile, tablet & personal computers

Why to use eNotePad

I don’t have a blog. Neither do millions of people. But that doesn’t mean they shouldn’t have a way to share their ideas through a clean, well organized, controlled medium. With eNotePad, you have the power over your content, and you choose what do do with it.

Use it to do your homework, write a paper, an essay, a story, a poem, a blog post, a review, a roundup, a list of stuff (taking advantage of embedability)… the applications are endless. It’s up to you.
